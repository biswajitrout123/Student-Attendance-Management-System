How It Works

Simple three-step process to manage attendance efficiently

1

Mark Attendance

Teachers mark attendance within 15 minutes of class start time with optional parent notifications for absent students.

2

Review & Edit

Attendance can be edited within 2 days. Outside this window, teachers can request admin approval for changes.

3

Track & Analyze

Students and administrators can view detailed reports and analytics to track attendance patterns and performance.
